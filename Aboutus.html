FastCheck

FastCheck was founded in 2017 to help people enhace their restaurant experience and make it faster. FastCheck had a monthly average of 24 million unique visitors who visited FastCheck via the FastCheck app and 65 million unique visitors who visited FastCheck via mobile web in Q4 2016.* Users have written more than 121 million reviews by the end of Q4 2016 In addition to reviews, you can use FastCheck to find events, lists and to talk with other Users. Every business owner (or manager) can setup a free account to post photos and message their customers. Fastcheck makes money by selling ads to local businesses - you’ll see these clearly labeled "FastCheck Ads" around the site. Paying advertisers can never change or re-order their reviews. FastCheck uses automated software to recommend the most helpful and reliable reviews for the FastCheck community among the millions we get. The software looks at dozens of different signals, including various measures of quality, reliability, and activity on FastCheck. The process has nothing to do with whether a business advertises on FastCheck or not. Learn more here. You can access FastCheck via iPhone, Android, and more - see the full list of mobile apps here. The Local FasCheck brings locals updates on the latest and greatest business openings And other happenings.
* Source: "Users" as measured by Google Analytics
